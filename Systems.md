VRFusion Software Compatibility Statement

【Comprehensive Cross-Platform Support】
VRFusion aims to provide a seamless cross-device experience, ensuring users can easily access the world's largest VR content ecosystem:

Official Store (VRFusion Store):
Natively supports all apps and games in the VRFusion Store, and is deeply optimized for native high resolution and Pancake Lens.

PC Streaming Compatibility (SteamVR / OpenXR):
Fully compatible with the SteamVR platform via VR-Link high-speed cable or Wi-Fi 6E wireless streaming. Users can directly run thousands of PC VR titles (such as Half-Life: Alyx).

Third-Party Application Support:
Supports the OpenXR industry standard protocol, ensuring perfect compatibility with most independent developer software and enterprise-level collaboration tools (such as Unity, Unreal Engine plugins).

Mobile Connectivity:
The dedicated VRFusion app supports iOS (15.0+) and Android (10.0+) systems for device settings, screen projection sharing, and social function management.
